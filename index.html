A simple potion to raise the dead.

From a qualified neighbourhood witch.

Perfect for the new and experienced necromancers.

Ingredients:
- Nightshade Extract – harvested under a new moon.
- A sprinkle of Ghoul’s Ash – remains of a burned undead.
- 1/2 cup Ectoplasm – collected from a tormented spirit.
- 3 drops from the blood of the living dead.
- 5 Bone Flower Petals – a purple flower that only grows in grave soil.
- 2 cups Soul Amber – crystallized soul energy.

Instructions:
On a blood moon, in a large cauldron (must be cast iron), combine all dry ingredients and mix thoroughly. On high heat, add the ectoplasm and wet ingredients while stirring constantly for four hours, chanting:
"mures inclusi essent pedes".
After four hours, let sit for 3 days. Then transfer to as many glass vials as needed. (Make sure to label correctly to avoid mixing with other spells.)
